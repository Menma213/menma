I noticed the game revolving around the basic missions so i had to spice them up to make them atleast a little interesting.  So! The game is a full on RPG now. It has a map, you're free to go anywhere you want on the map as long as you beat the areas Guardian. Guardians are bosses so they might be pretty tough, but its worth it. There's gonna be 9 total regions:
Land of Fire (You start here, Tier 1) Basic rewards and material drop.
The Village of Sound (Tier 2) x2 of the rewards & materials dropped.
The Country of Rain (Tier 3) x3
Land of Wind (Tier 4) x4
and so on until 
The valley of the End (Tier 9) x9

Note that these numbers might sound insane and they actually are. The brank you do in Land of Fire will give u 100 exp but the same in valley of end(different npc) will give you 900. The catch is that the Guardians between Tiers are omega broken. So do not expect yourself to easily climb to  Tier 9's.
All of the above stuff are done through these commands: /travel challenge, /travel move, /travel view, /map.
